About

I am a 2nd year GW4 BioMed PhD candidate at the University of Bristol.

My project is focused on the relationship between immune cell counts, type 2 diabetes, and colorectal cancer.

I use R, Python, and occasionally C++ to combine methods in epidemiology and data science that allow me to present many interesting results.

Bristol is a great city to do a PhD in. It has a vibrant and diverse community, with many interesting and unique places to visit.

This is a personal website where I will share some of the projects I am working on during my trip to the outer boundaries of what is known in science.
