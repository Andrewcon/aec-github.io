About

I am a 3rd year GW4BioMed PhD candidate at the MRC Integrative Epidemiology Unit, University of Bristol.

My project is focused on the relationship between immune cell counts and disease.

I use R, Python and statistics to combine methods in population genetics and data science that allow me to conduct cutting-edge research.

Bristol is a great city to do a PhD in. It has a vibrant and diverse community, with many interesting and unique places to visit.

This is a personal website where I share some of the projects I am working on during my trip to the outer boundaries of what is known in science.
